Rainbow Dash was born in Cloudsdale and was the only child of Bow Hothoof and Windy Whistles.
In her childhood, she knew "The Girls", a circle of friends Windy Whistles was apart of, even though she seemed to be annoyed of them.

Rainbow Dash has a pet tortoise named Tank, whom she chooses out of Fluttershy's offered animals.
She represents the element of loyalty.
She later marries Soarin and has a son named Gust.

Rainbow Dash is a pegasus who is responsible for maintaining the weather and clearing the skies in Ponyville.
Enhanced Fly:
Rainbow demonstrated remarkable speed and agility in the air, Rainbow Dash has proven herself to be a capable and accomplished flier.
